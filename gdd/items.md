# GDD: Items

---

Two item categories exist: consumables (single-use, typically restore HP or grant a temporary effect) and persistent items (key items, badges, and permanent upgrades that remain in the player's inventory indefinitely).

> **→ TDD note (items):** HP restoration values and temporary effect magnitudes are not yet specified. All values marked TBD require a balancing pass before implementation.

## Persistent Items

Remain in the player's inventory once acquired. Divided into key items (required for progression), permanent upgrades (passive stat effects), and badges (collectibles).

---

### Key Items

Required to access locked areas. Cannot be used or discarded.

| Item | Effect | Acquisition |
| ---- | ------ | ----------- |
| ID Card | Grants access to the school's buildings | Prof. Schwaartz — she tells you that you left it in her class |
| Secret Code | Grants access to the courtyard | Vec Tor — given after defeating him in battle |

---

### Permanent Upgrades

Passive effects applied immediately on acquisition; persist for the remainder of the session.

| Item | Effect | Acquisition |
| ---- | ------ | ----------- |
| Star Gourd | Permanent exp boost | Marcellus — reward after defeating him |
| Emotional Support Pickle | Defense boost (TBD) | Beckett |
| Campus Swag Bag | "it's full of useless garbage" — no mechanical effect | Found in one of the classrooms |

---

### Badges

Collectible items with no combat effect. Awarded for completing optional objectives. Badges show up on your backpack after you've earned them

| Badge | Acquisition Condition |
| ----- | --------------------- |
| Meal Lovers Badge | Find all food items and speak to Casey |
| Music League Badge | Awarded by Lionel |
| Dialecters Badge | Awarded by Suzanna after defeating all three linguistics professors (Schwaartz, Syntaxa, and Composita) |
| Community Badge | Awarded by Ruby after speaking to every NPC, including Parsemore |
| Acorn Badge | Found under a bench in the overworld when you sit down |
