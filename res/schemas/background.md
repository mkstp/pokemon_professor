# Schema: Background

File: `background.md`

Each entry follows this structure:

```
Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). *Title of work.* Publisher. URL (if available)

Annotation paragraph (4–5 sentences): (1) what the source argues or establishes;
(2) its methodology or approach; (3) its key contribution to the field;
(4) its specific relevance to this framework.
```

**Citation format:** APA 7th edition throughout.

**Links:** Include a verified URL or DOI where available. Mark unverified links as `[URL to be verified]`. Do not fabricate links.

**Annotation content:** Annotations must address relevance to this framework explicitly — not just what the source says, but why it matters here.

**Organisation:** Alphabetical by author surname. Single flat list — no domain grouping.
